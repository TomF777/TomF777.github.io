Lifestyle

What is healthy life style ?

A way of living that helps you enjoy more aspects fo your life. Health is not just about avoiding a disease or illness. It is about physical, mental and social weel-being too. The three main components of being physically fit are:

  • Stamina
  • Strength
  • Flexibility
Physical activity is necessary to stimulate the body's own natural maintenance and repair system.

It is important to enjoy the food you eat. You can build a healthy and balanced diet by choosing your food as if you are building a Food Pyramid:

  • Potatoes, Bread, Rice and Cereals - Eat Most
  • Vegetables and Fruit - Eat More
  • Meat, Fish and Pulses - Eat Moderately
  • Milk and Dairy Products - Eat Moderately
  • Fats, Oil, Sugar and Salt - Eat Less
